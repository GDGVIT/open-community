Our programme

GDG VIT Vellore, with WTM (Women Techmakers) conducts many events. We host the I/O Extended event by Google and also the Devfest and a hackathon annually in the month of October. We also conduct other informative sessions throughout the year.
Other than this, we will offer a two-credit course starting from July. This course will span over 90 hours till December and includes the following:

  • Weekly sessions on Hardware, Python, Web Development
  • Ideation sessions
  • A Hackathon
  • Tech talks and mini info sessions
  • A mini project
